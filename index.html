Who we are

Our story

Sherpa is an on-demand luggage delivery service transporting bags between homes, hotels, Airbnbs and airports, stations. And back.

Our team of globetrotters is working to eliminate travel anxiety -- so wherever you're going, you spend less time planning around your bags, and more time on what's important.

Founded in London.

FAQs

How do I know it will be safe?

Our Sherpa drivers go through an intensive interview process and background check, so our customers know their items are in safe hands. Your luggage is also secured with a locked band with a code that you create.

Where can I find my Sherpa in the airport or train station?

Your confirmation email will contain your exact meeting point in the airport or train station You can find exactly where to meet your Sherpa driver, whether in the airport or train station, here.

How can I get in contact with my Sherpa?

Your Sherpa driver will text you directly to let you know when they are on their way. If you have any questions in the meantime, you can always call our 24/7 customer service at +447927979004 or email hello@getyoursherpa.com

What if something happens with my bag?

Sherpa has never lost a bag – but in the unlikely event that something happened, bags are each insured up to £1,000.

What if my travel plans change?

Let us know one hour in advance of your pick-up or meeting time, and we’ll change your booking for free.

What if I am not able to pick up my bags before my flight or train?

OWe will arrange for your item to be rush-delivered to you free of charge, no matter where you’re going next.

Do you deliver to my address?

We currently deliver between London zones 1-3, London airports (Heathrow, Gatwick and London City Airport), and all major London train stations (Paddington, Victoria, King’s Cross/St Pancras, London Bridge, Waterloo, Liverpool Street).
